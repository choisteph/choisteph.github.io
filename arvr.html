immersive theatre

winter 2019 - intro to ar/vr

ar prototype video

My team (Kangning Chen, Stephanie Choi, Sunny Kim, Vincent Qiu) created a VR and AR Immersive/Augmented theatre experience. Our goal was to create an immersive visual-theatre experience in which audience members can freely navigate around a scene. We created two separate projects; one was created in VR and the other was created in AR.

achieved goals

  • VR physical prototype using multimedia
  • VR digital prototype using paper drawing
  • VR digital prototype using 360-degree photos, sound, and 3D models in A-Frame
  • AR physical prototype using multimedia
  • AR digital prototype using markers and Vuforia Engine for Unity
